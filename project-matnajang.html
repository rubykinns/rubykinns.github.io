About the Project

During the 'Service Design' course, I created Matnajang (맛나장) — a platform service that aims to enhance profit for both restaurants and grocery suppliers by connecting them directly through a mobile application.

The name "Matnajang" comes from the Korean phrase meaning "Let's Meet Together," reflecting the platform's core mission of bringing food industry stakeholders closer. I was responsible for branding, prototyping, and all graphic design work, as well as translating all content in the mobile application prototype.

Naming

Matnajang = 맛나장 (Korean). The name means "Let's Meet Together" — a mobile application prototype designed to enhance profit for both restaurants and grocery suppliers.
