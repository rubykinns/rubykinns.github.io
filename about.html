About

Bridging behavior
and design

I'm Yeeun Kim (Ruby), a Ph.D. Candidate in Industrial Engineering at Penn State University. I specialize in mixed-methods UX and human factors research — the kind that goes beyond preference surveys to understand how people actually think and behave.

I've led studies with over 1,700 participants across consumer platforms (Booking.com), AI-driven health applications (HAII Corporation), and safety-critical systems sponsored by General Motors and the AAA Foundation for Traffic Safety.

My research examines cognitive workload, mental models, decision-making, and trust — translating complex behavioral data into design recommendations that are both evidence-based and actionable.
